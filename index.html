Eva Ericsson LCSW

Insight to understand. Courage to change.

Eva helps individuals and couples in the western suburbs better understand the issues that limit their potential for happiness, then encourages them to take small, brave steps toward positive change.

Using a variety of approaches to achieve this “better understanding,” Eva also draws on more than 30 years of therapeutic clinical experience. Her advice and guidance is grounded in a realistic view of the stresses of modern life, as well as a huge reservoir of compassion for her fellow human beings trying to navigate it successfully.

Eva welcomes adolescents, adults and couples of all races, faiths and sexual orientations.
